Random polygon generation

Home




In this lab, we deal with the random generation of simple polygons on a given set of points. Since no polynomial-time solution is known for the uniformly random generation of simple polygons, we focus on heuristics that offer a good time complexity and still generate a rich variety of different polygons. Also considered approaching minimum area polygonization.
